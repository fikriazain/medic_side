About

Our mission is to provide support and companionship to hemodialysis patients through an intelligent, accessible chatbot. Designed to assist patients during and between dialysis sessions, our chatbot helps monitor health, answer questions, and offer guidance based on individual needs. By integrating AI and healthcare expertise, we aim to improve the quality of life for dialysis patients, offering them personalized, around-the-clock assistance whenever needed..
